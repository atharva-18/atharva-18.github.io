Contouring Model Predictive Control for RWD Race-cars

At DJS Racing, the Formula Student team of Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, we are building one of the first driverless formula student race-car. In this presentation, we are presenting to you the improved control architecture of our autonomous system for the year 2021.

It is an extension of Contouring Model Predictive Control for RWD cars with a differential, using the HPIPM NLP solver. Globalrace-trajectory optimization for shortest time was done using Time-Optimal Trajectory Planning (Christ et al., 2019).
